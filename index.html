About

Writing with empathy, depth, and clarity.

Orugboh Tosan is a writer and storyteller with a background in English and Literary Studies. Her work explores faith, identity, emotion, family, culture, healing, and human transformation.

Through Tee Writes, she helps clients shape books, stories, personal narratives, reflective pieces, and polished content with care for voice, meaning, and emotional truth.

  • Style: Literary, warm, reflective, faith-aware
  • Work mode: Remote collaborations from Nigeria
